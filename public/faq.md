# CareerNow — FAQ

## What is CareerNow?
An AI-powered career platform that combines resume building, job search, interview practice, and application tracking in one workflow.

## Is there a free version?
Yes. The Free plan is permanent and includes the resume builder preview, ATS checker, and 5 job searches per day.

## How much does Pro cost?
$12/month, or $9.60/month when billed yearly (20% discount).

## Are resumes ATS-friendly?
Yes. All 6 templates use clean structure, standard fonts, and bullet-pointed achievements that pass major ATS systems including Greenhouse, Lever, Workday, and Taleo.

## Which job boards are included?
CareerNow aggregates listings from 14+ boards including LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor, RemoteOK, We Work Remotely, Remote.co, AngelList, Wellfound, ZipRecruiter, and more.

## Can I download my resume?
Free users can preview but not download. Pro and Enterprise users get unlimited PDF, DOCX, and PNG exports.

## Does it write cover letters?
Yes. The AI tailors each cover letter to the job description. Free users get 1 per day; Pro and Enterprise are unlimited.

## How does Smart Apply work?
Smart Apply parses your resume, optimizes it for ATS, generates a tailored cover letter, and matches you to live jobs — all in one flow. Pro and Enterprise only.

## Is my data private?
Yes. Resume data is stored securely in your account and never shared with third parties. You can export or delete it at any time.

## Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There are no contracts. Cancel from your account settings; access continues until the end of your billing period. A 30-day money-back guarantee also applies.

## Do you support teams?
Yes. The Enterprise plan supports up to 25 team members with shared analytics, custom branding, and white-label options.

## How do I contact support?
Pro users get priority support; Enterprise users get a dedicated account manager. Visit https://careernow.xyz to get in touch.
