# CareerNow — Pricing

## Plans

### Free — $0 / forever
For exploring the platform.
- 3 resume templates
- AI Resume Builder (preview only, no downloads)
- Resume Checker & Grader
- 1 cover letter per day
- 5 job searches per day
- Skills Assessment Quiz

### Pro — $12 / month (or $9.60/month billed yearly)
For active job seekers.
- All 6 resume templates
- Unlimited PDF, DOCX, and PNG downloads
- AI Resume Optimizer & ATS Scoring
- AI Interview Practice
- Unlimited cover letters
- 50 job searches per day (1,500 / month cap)
- Smart Apply Pipeline
- LinkedIn Profile Optimizer
- Company Research Tool
- Job alerts and application tracking
- Priority support

### Enterprise — $39 / month (or $31.20/month billed yearly)
For coaches, agencies, and teams.
- Everything in Pro
- Unlimited job searches
- Up to 25 team members
- Team analytics dashboard
- Custom resume branding
- Portfolio Builder
- Career Path Planner
- API access
- Dedicated account manager
- White-label options

## Trial
No free trial. The Free plan is permanent and lets users evaluate every tool before upgrading.

## Billing
- Monthly or yearly subscriptions (yearly saves 20%)
- Processed by Polar.sh
- 30-day money-back guarantee on Pro and Enterprise
- Cancel anytime — no setup fees

## Who each plan is for
- **Free**: students, casual browsers, curious users
- **Pro**: anyone actively applying to jobs
- **Enterprise**: career coaches, recruiters, agencies, and corporate teams
